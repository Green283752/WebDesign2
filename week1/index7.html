Lisa Frank is one of the most iconic 1990's commercial artists. Her work was all over my school supplies as a child. I like her color schemes although some of it is very cluttered. Looking back now, I seem to be critiquing all of the art I see because that's what I do now. This is the new me. I'm a walking critic. Lisa, may your work live on through to the present day even if I think it's too cluttered and needs more white space.

Lisa's color palette's are definitely what attracted me to her art. I like colorful art and designs. However, I'm working on making my art balanced with color. To have a distinctly bright palette is sometimes shocking to the naked eye. Therefore, I feel that it must be in balance with the white space, composition, focus, and layout of the page.
