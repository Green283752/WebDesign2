Wine Bar

We pride ourselves on our boutique wine list. Our wine vendors are passionate about getting us fine wines at reasonable prices. French wines in particular are a great value at the bistro. Not many restaurants offer a fine Sancerre by the glass. We cover many regions in France – Cote du Rhone, Bordeaux (St. Emillion, Pomerol, Margaux), Provence, Alsace to name a few. As for the California wines, we like to stay as local as possible with great whites and reds from Santa Barbara and western Paso Robles. We even have a Topanga wine from our friends Jerry O’Connell and Rebecca Romijn ‘Wagon Wheel’ grown in Calabasas!
The wine bar provides two beers on tap.

Currently offering:

Pranqster & Scrimshaw

Our bottled beers are boutique. Organic wheat beers from Germany and France. I.P.A. from Anderson Valley, Ca. Samuel Smith organic lager from England. You may discover a new favorite!
Bar designed by Wallace & Hines: custom bar company is known for it’s fine craftsmanship and attention to detail in all it’s installed bars across the USA. This bar is the first of it’s kind made by the company for The Canyon Bistro. Starting off as a portable bar on wheels, we decided to take the casters off and increase the size of the bar counter so we can entertain diners and as well as wine enthusiasts. Integrated dual zone wine & beer refrigerator houses white wines at proper temperature and also houses two beer kegs at a colder temp. Designed and built in Blue Lake, California.
